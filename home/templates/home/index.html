Thaiboxing

Thai boxing is Thailand’s national sport and one of the toughest martial arts there is. This is because you have to use punches, kicks, elbows and knees, which give you the best posibilties for a fullbody workout. In addition, it requires extra of your concentration due to the physical and technical aspect.

About Boxing gym

Boxing Gym is a unique and original place that offers fitness boxing and thaiboxing. There is probably no other place like Boxing Gym in the whole world. As soon as you enter the door, you get caught and seized by the atmosphere. The premises are rustic and characterized by many years of workout. We are not afraid to call us unique. Everyone is welcome. Even though you haven't boxed or trained before, you will be well received and we'll do our best to assist you with the exercises.
You participate entirely on your own terms and you set the pace and intensity of the training.
So, if you want to try something new, Boxing Gym is something for you.
